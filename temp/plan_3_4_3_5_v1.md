### Gap papers — what would fill the gap but doesn't exist

This is a §3.5-synthesis item, not §3.4 body content. Categories of "missing" work:

1. **No peer-reviewed AoE2 paper uses gradient boosted trees or random forests with rigorous temporal cross-validation.** The one peer-reviewed AoE2 prediction paper (CetinTas2023) uses Naive Bayes and Decision Trees.

6. **No AoE2 replay-based prediction paper** — a gap noted as a consequence of missing BWAPI-equivalent tooling for AoE2 until recently (AgeAlyser_2 mentioned in the seed bibliography, line 170–171).
